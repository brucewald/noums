How it works

The pause is the skill. We help you find it.

Fillers are what we say to fill a thinking pause. noums makes those pauses visible so a confident silence replaces the reflex.

01

Get asked

A camera or a friendly on-screen interviewer poses a real question — behavioral, casual, or one you bring yourself.

02

Answer out loud

Speak your response naturally. A live meter counts fillers as you go, so you feel the moment you reach for one.

03

See the tape

Get a fluency score, a filler breakdown, pacing, and one concrete thing to work on next time.
